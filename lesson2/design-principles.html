Music App - Whitespace

www.musicapp.co

There are a many aspects of good web design, and whitespace is certainly one of them. Whitespace is the empty space around design elements such as images, text, columns, etc. It is that blank space that makes layouts clean and sophisticated.

Amazon - Fitts Law

www.amazon.co.au

Fitts' law states that the amount of time required for a person to move a pointer (e.g., mouse cursor) to a target area is a function of the distance to the target divided by the size of the target. Thus, the longer the distance and the smaller the target's size, the longer it takes. Because this is a commercial (shop) website, the ability to quickly quick from an item, and be presented with lots of items at once is important.

Tico Shaving - Rule of Thirds

www.ticoshaving.com

The rule of thirds is a "rule of thumb" for composing visual images such as designs, films, paintings, and photographs. The guideline proposes that an image should be imagined as divided into nine equal parts by two equally spaced horizontal lines and two equally spaced vertical lines, and that important compositional elements should be placed along these lines or their intersections. The web page should look balanced and pleasing to the eye.
