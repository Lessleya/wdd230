Personas

Persona:

Business Owner

Name:

Tom Jones

Job Title:

Owner of Garage

Environment:

He is comfortable using a computer and uses email as his primary communication. He doesn't have a deep understanding of the web and doesn't like to spend lots of time browsing without purpose.

Persona:

Timaru City Resident

Name:

Susy Smith

Job Title:

Retired

Environment:

She spends time online while the kids are at school or after they are asleep. Most of her internet browsing is for shopping, either to buy online or to search for local places to get the things she needs to care for her family.

Scenarios

  • What events are happening in the community in the next few weeks?
  • How can I get involved in opportunities to help and serve here in College Station?
  • How can I help bring traffic to my small business?
  • Where can I find out about things to do in College Station?
  • What is important to College Station's local community?
