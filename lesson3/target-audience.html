Site Plan - for "We Love Timaru" Chamber of Commerce website

    Home Target-Audience Color-Scheme Typography

Personas

Persona:

Business Owner

Name:

Tom Jones

Job Title:

Owner of Garage

Environment:

This person uses all technology and knows more and more people use their mobile phones to access information. They understand a user-friendly website should also be accessible to all users. Such users understand that if information is organised and well presented on the Chamber of Commerce website more success will follow. Lastly, this person is busy and acknowledges the average Internet user skims through site pages, so they need to steer users to the right information quickly.

Persona:

Timaru City Resident

Name:

Susy Smith

Job Title:

Retired

Environment:

This resident of Timaru is new to this area, so needs to find out what services are in this business and residential community, and the features of those services to assist their own family and own community involvement.

Scenarios

  • What events are happening in the community in the next few weeks?
  • How can this and other businesses and residents find benefits from using this website and joining the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What website features and opportunities exist to benefit small business?
  • Recognition that informing as the first step in planting opportunities, so a longer term view/use of the website/Chamber will be needed?
  • What current and future features are the businesses and residents wanting?
